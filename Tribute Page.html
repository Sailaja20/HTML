Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs

The Inventor of Apple Inc.


Early Life :

Steven Jobs was born February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, and was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs. He grew up with one sister, Patty. Paul Jobs was a machinist and fixed cars as a hobby. Jobs remembers his father as being very skilled at working with his hands.

A List of Steve's Awards:

  • 1985: National Medal of Technology and Innovation.

  • 1987: Jefferson Award for Greatest Public Service.

  • 2002: PGA Vanguard Award.

  • 2011: Bravo Otto - Social Media Star.

  • 2012: Grammy Trustees Award.

  • 2013: Posthumously inducted as a Disney Legend

  • 2017: Steve Jobs Theatre opens at Apple Park
